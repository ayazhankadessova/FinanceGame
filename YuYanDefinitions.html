How much do you make and how much do you spend?

What is saving?
Definition: Savings is the income not spent, or deferred consumption.
What is income?
Definition: Income refers to cash inflows, consisting primarily of salaries and wages.
Money received, especially on a regular basis, for work or through investments. E.g., working part time, or full time jobs, you get paid (that is income). Income can also be one time such as lai see money etc.
What is expense?
Definition: Expenses are cash outflows that sustain our scale of living. Purchase of investment assets is not consider as expenses.
Expenses can be grouped into: Inflexible Expenses, Flexible Expenses.
Inflexible expenses
Definition: (fixed expenses) are expenses that are hard to control in the short run. Example of perfectly inflexible expenses are mortgage payment and car loan payment.. Other inflexible expenses are not fixed in amount from period to period but still difficult to control.
Flexible Expenses
Definition: (variable expenses) are expenses that are generally controllable in the short run. Example of flexible expenses are leisure and entertainment expenses.
