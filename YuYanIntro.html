Yu Yan's Story

Meet YuYan, a determined and hardworking freshman at Hong Kong Baptist University. With her birthday fast approaching, she has set her sights on a goal that many might consider lofty - a visit to the magical world of DisneyLand.

But there's a catch - YuYan wants to pay for this trip on her own, and with her high expenses, she knows that this will require some serious financial discipline.

As a result, YuYan has decided to embark on a journey of financial responsibility, as she learns the importance of good saving habits, budgeting, and distinguishing between her needs and wants. She has set herself a goal of saving 640 Hong Kong dollars, which is no easy feat, but with her determination and grit, she is up for the challenge.

Join YuYan on her journey as she discovers the importance of budgeting and managing her expenses, while also learning to distinguish between her needs and wants. With a newfound appreciation for financial responsibility, and her sights set on her dream destination, YuYan is ready to take charge of her finances and make her birthday wish come true.
