Envelope Method

An amazing way to manage your cash flow and control your spending - Envelope Method! This simple and easy-to-follow system is a game changer for your finances. The beauty of the Envelope Method is that it allows you to allocate your money in advance, which means that you have predetermined limits on how much you can spend in each category per month. This ensures that you stay within your budget and avoid overspending.
